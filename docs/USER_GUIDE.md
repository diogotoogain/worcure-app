# WORCURE MEMBERS - User Guide

## Welcome to WORCURE MEMBERS

WORCURE MEMBERS is your comprehensive wellness platform designed to help you achieve your health and wellness goals through structured modules and educational content.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up" to create your account. Choose from three membership tiers:
- **Free**: Access to basic features and 200 FLIX modules
- **Premium**: Access to all features and 500 FLIX modules
- **Elite**: Full access to all 720 FLIX modules plus premium support

### 2. Set Up Your Profile
Complete your profile with:
- Basic information (name, email)
- Health goals
- Fitness level
- Dietary preferences
- Notification settings

### 3. Explore Modules
Navigate to the Modules page to explore our 13 main features:

#### Wellness Core
Your central hub for tracking overall wellness metrics and daily check-ins.

#### Nutrition Tracker
Log your meals, track calories and macros, and get personalized nutrition recommendations.

#### Exercise Planner
Create workout plans, track exercises, and monitor your fitness progress.

#### Mental Health
Access meditation guides, mood tracking, and stress management tools.

#### Sleep Monitor
Track your sleep patterns and get recommendations for better rest.

#### Habit Builder
Build and maintain positive habits with streak tracking and reminders.

#### Goal Tracker
Set and track your wellness goals with milestone tracking.

#### Social Connect
Connect with other members, join challenges, and share your progress.

#### Education Hub
Access our library of wellness content, articles, and videos.

#### Analytics Dashboard
Visualize your progress with comprehensive charts and insights.

#### AI Assistant
Get personalized recommendations and instant support.

#### Community Forum
Join discussions and get support from the community.

#### Premium Features
Unlock advanced features (Premium and Elite members only).

## FLIX Learning Modules

### What is FLIX?
FLIX is our comprehensive educational system with 720 modules organized into 12 categories:

1. **Foundation (60 modules)**: Getting started essentials
2. **Health Tracking (60 modules)**: Monitoring your health metrics
3. **Fitness (60 modules)**: Exercise and workout guidance
4. **Nutrition (60 modules)**: Food and diet education
5. **Mental Wellness (60 modules)**: Mental health support
6. **Sleep (60 modules)**: Sleep optimization techniques
7. **Social (60 modules)**: Community and social features
8. **Gamification (60 modules)**: Rewards and achievements
9. **Analytics (60 modules)**: Data and insights
10. **Education (60 modules)**: Learning resources
11. **Integration (60 modules)**: Third-party connections
12. **Premium (60 modules)**: Exclusive content

### How to Use FLIX
1. Browse modules by category
2. Select a module to view its content
3. Complete modules to earn points and badges
4. Track your progress in the Dashboard

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my membership tier?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings.

**Q: How are points calculated?**
A: Points are earned through daily activities, module completions, and achieving goals.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Data Export to download your complete data.

**Q: Is there a family plan?**
A: Yes, Elite membership includes family access for up to 5 members.

**Q: How often should I use the app?**
A: We recommend daily check-ins, but use what works for your schedule.
